PNG with transparency works best

Only logos with a transparent background can be placed on your video — a logo with a solid background will appear as a visible box instead of just the artwork. Don't have a transparent version? Use the built-in tool below to make one.

Remove background

Click anywhere on the logo's background below to pick the color to remove. This is what makes the logo safe to place on video — the removed area becomes truly transparent, not just white.

Tip: click directly on the background color (not the artwork). Raise tolerance if some background remains; lower it if the logo itself starts disappearing.
