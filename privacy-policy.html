Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026.

1. Files you process

Videos and logo images you drop into LogoShield Studio are loaded directly into your browser's memory and processed on your own device using an in-browser video engine. These files are not uploaded to our servers as part of the editing or rendering flow, and we do not retain copies of them.

2. Information we do collect

Like most websites, our hosting and analytics providers may automatically log standard technical data such as IP address, browser type, device type, and pages visited, for the purpose of operating and improving the site and diagnosing issues.

3. Advertising and cookies

This site displays advertising to remain free to use. Advertising partners (and any analytics providers we use) may set cookies or similar technologies in your browser to measure ad performance and, where applicable, personalize ads. You can control or delete cookies through your browser settings, and many ad networks offer opt-out tools.

4. Third parties

We do not sell your files. We may work with third-party advertising or analytics providers who operate under their own privacy policies; we encourage you to review those where relevant.

5. Children's privacy

LogoShield Studio is not directed at children under 13 and we do not knowingly collect personal information from them.

6. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy as the tool evolves. Material changes will be reflected by updating the date above.

7. Contact

Questions about this policy can be sent through our contact page.
